Biography

Tetsuya Nomura was born in K≈çchi Prefecture, Japan on October 8, 1970 (age 50). Nomura's passion for art began when he was just 3 years old. Encourage by his father to begin learning digital art, Nomura began playing video games. His early influences would eventually point him towards his eventual destiny. Nomura was influenced by the Dragon Quest and Final Fantasy SNES titles.

Nomura's early career with Square would begin as a debugger on Final Fantasy IV. Nomura became an artist on Final Fantasy V before eventually becoming the graphic director on Final Fantasy VI. Nomura's characters would go on to become some of the most recognizable character designs in gaming history. Practically anyone who has ever picked up a video game has heard the name Cloud Strife. Even Disney was convinced to work with the enigmatic Nomura in the creation of Kingdom Hearts, which to many is still one of the most bizarre partnerships in media.
